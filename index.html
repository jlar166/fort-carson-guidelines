    Introduction

    The following guidelines have been developed and approved by the FCFD and EACH EMS in conjunction with a working group representing FCFD, EACH EMS, AMR, CSFD, SCRETAC, SECRETAC and P2PRETAC.

    Purpose: These guidelines delineate the expected standard of care and acts allowed for EMT, EMT-IV and paramedic providers under local medical direction.

    Key Principles

    • Guidelines are evidence-based where possible
    • Reviewed on a six-month basis
    • No substitute for sound clinical judgment
    • Contact medical control when situations arise outside guidelines
