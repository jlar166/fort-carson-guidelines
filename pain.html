Pain Management (B004)

Objectives

The objective of pain management is not complete relief of all pain, but rather to make a patient's pain tolerable to allow for assessment, treatment, and transport.

Medication Options

Mild to Moderate Pain:
  • Acetaminophen IV/IO:
    • Adult <50kg: 500mg
    • Adult >50kg: 1,000mg
    • Pediatric: 15mg/kg (MAX 1000mg)
Moderate to Severe Pain:
  • Ketamine:
    • Adult: 30mg IV/IO or 50mg IM/IN (up to 3 doses)
    • Pediatric >7: 0.3mg/kg IV/IO or 0.5mg/kg IM/IN
  • Fentanyl:
    • Adult: 50-100mcg IV/IO or IN/IM
    • Pediatric: 1mcg/kg IV/IO or IN/IM
  • Hydromorphone:
    • Adult: 0.5mg IV/IO or IM (NOT REPEATED)
    • Pediatric: NOT allowed
Precautions:
  • Monitor ECG, SpO2, EtCO2, and BP continuously
  • Consider Â½ typical dosing in elderly patients
  • Watch for respiratory depression
  • Pain medications should only be given to hemodynamically stable patients
