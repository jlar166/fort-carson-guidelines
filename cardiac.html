Cardiac Emergencies (D001-D008)

Acute Coronary Syndrome (ACS)

Cardiac Alert Criteria:
  • ACS symptoms AND
  • 12-lead showing:
    • ST elevation â‰¥1mm in 2+ contiguous leads, OR
    • ST elevation â‰¥1mm in aVR with widespread depression, OR
    • 2mm ST depression in V1-V2 (posterior), OR
    • Computer interpretation of "ACUTE MI"
Treatment:
  • Aspirin: 162-324mg PO (Adult only)
  • Establish vascular access
  • If signs of poor perfusion: Fluid therapy 250-500mL NS
  • Consider pain management
  • Notify receiving hospital ASAP - request CARDIAC ALERT

Bradydysrhythmia

If Symptomatic with signs of poor perfusion:
  • Atropine:
    • Adult: 0.5mg IV/IO every 3min (MAX 3mg)
    • Peds: 0.02mg/kg IV/IO (MIN 0.1mg, MAX single dose 0.5mg)
  • Transcutaneous Pacing:
    • Adult: 60 bpm, increase energy until capture
    • Peds: 80 bpm, increase energy until capture
  • Epinephrine Infusion: 1mg in 1,000mL NS wide open to gravity

Tachydysrhythmia

Narrow Complex (QRS <0.12):< /strong>
  • Vagal Maneuvers (if stable)
  • Adenosine:
    • Adult: 6-12mg rapid IV/IO with 20mL NS flush
    • Peds: 0.1mg/kg (max 12mg), repeat at 0.2mg/kg
  • Diltiazem (irregular):
    • Adult: 20mg IV/IO over 2-5min, repeat at 25mg after 15min
Wide Complex (QRS >0.12):
  • Amiodarone (regular monomorphic):
    • Adult: 150mg IV/IO over 10min
  • Magnesium (polymorphic/torsades):
    • Adult: 2g slow IV/IO over 10min
If Signs of Poor Perfusion:
  • Synchronized Cardioversion
  • Consider sedation with Midazolam first if stable

Medical Cardiac Arrest

Adult >19 Years:
  • Withhold Resuscitation if ALL present:
    • Unwitnessed arrest AND
    • Age â‰¥73 years AND
    • PEA/Asystole (non-shockable)
  • Chest Compressions: 2 minutes cycles
  • Airway: First 3 cycles passive O2, then PPV
  • For VF/VT: Defibrillate at MAX joules
  • Consider TOR after: 4 cycles (8min) AND EtCO2 <10< /li>
Pediatric <18 Years:
  • Epinephrine 1:10,000:
    • >13: 1mg IV/IO (MAX 3 doses)
    • <13: 0.01mg/kg IV/IO
  • Amiodarone:
    • >13: 300mg IV/IO, then 150mg
    • <13: 5mg/kg IV/IO (up to 3 doses)
  • Defibrillation:
    • >13: MAX joules
    • <13: 4 J/kg
  • Consider TOR after: 10 cycles (20min)
