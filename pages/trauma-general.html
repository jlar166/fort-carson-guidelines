Standard Trauma Care (F001)

General Principles

  • Scene times should be 10 minutes or less if possible
  • Invasive treatments should be performed en route
  • Early notification to receiving facility
  • EtCO2 SHOULD be utilized in any major trauma
  • Avoid hypothermia - maintain patient warmth

Initial Assessment

  • Consider C-spine precautions (see Spinal Motion Restriction)
  • Maintain patent airway and OXYGENATION
  • Monitor EtCO2 and obtain VS
  • Complete neurologic exam
  • Control bleeding:
    • Direct pressure and/or
    • Tourniquet and/or
    • Hemostatic agent and/or
    • Pressure point and/or
    • Pelvic binder and/or
    • Wound packing (junctional injuries ONLY)
  • Establish vascular access if clinically indicated
  • Treat for shock if clinically indicated
