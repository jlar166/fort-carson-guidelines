Cerebral Vascular Accident (E002)

BE-FAST Stroke Screening

  • Balance - sudden loss of balance or coordination
  • Eyes - sudden change in vision or abnormal pupils
  • Face - does one side droop?
  • Arms - does one arm drift?
  • Speech - is speech slurred or strange?
  • Time - when was last known normal?

Stroke Alert Activation & Transport

Last Known Normal <4 hours:
  • Transport to closest facility that provides TPA
  • Facilities: St. Francis, Memorial North, Memorial Central, Penrose Main
Last Known Normal 4-24 hours:
  • Transport to facility with intracranial intervention
  • Facilities: Memorial Central, Penrose Main
Last Known Normal >24 hours:
  • Transport to closest appropriate facility
