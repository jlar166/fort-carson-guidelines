    üíä Drug Quick Reference Cards

    Quick reference cards for commonly used medications. Click a category to filter.

    Epinephrine 1:10,000
    Class Sympathomimetic
    Adult Dose 1mg IV/IO q3-5min
    Peds Dose 0.01mg/kg IV/IO
    Route IV, IO
    Level EMT
    Amiodarone
    Class Antiarrhythmic
    Adult Dose 300mg IV/IO, then 150mg
    Peds Dose 5mg/kg IV/IO (max 300mg)
    Indication VF/pVT unresponsive to shock
    Level Paramedic
    Albuterol
    Class Beta-2 Agonist
    Adult Dose 2.5-5mg nebulized
    Peds Dose 2.5mg nebulized
    May Repeat Every 10-15 min PRN
    Level EMT
    Fentanyl
    Class Opioid Analgesic
    Adult Dose 25-100mcg IV/IN
    Peds Dose 1mcg/kg (max 50mcg)
    Onset 1-2 min IV, 5-10 min IN
    Level Paramedic
    Caution: Hypotension, respiratory depression
    Midazolam (Versed)
    Class Benzodiazepine
    Seizure Dose 5-10mg IN/IM
    Peds Seizure 0.2mg/kg IN/IM (max 10mg)
    Sedation 2.5-5mg IV (titrate)
    Level Paramedic
    Diphenhydramine (Benadryl)
    Class Antihistamine
    Adult Dose 25-50mg IV/IM
    Peds Dose 1mg/kg IV/IM (max 50mg)
    Route IV, IO, IM
    Level Paramedic
    Dextrose 10%
    Class Carbohydrate
    Adult Dose 25g (250mL) IV
    Peds Dose 0.5g/kg = 5mL/kg D10
    Indication Hypoglycemia (BG <60)
    Level EMT-IV
    Naloxone (Narcan)
    Class Opioid Antagonist
    Adult Dose 0.4-2mg IV/IN/IM
    Peds Dose 0.1mg/kg (max 2mg)
    May Repeat Every 2-3 min PRN
    Level EMT
    Note: Goal is respiratory effort, not full reversal
