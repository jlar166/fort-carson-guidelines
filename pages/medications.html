Medication Overview (H000)

Principles of Administration

  • Closed loop communication
  • Cross-check with another provider when possible
  • Verify appropriateness including routes
  • Use Pediatric Field Guide for pediatric dosing

Medication Errors

The system follows a "Just Culture" approach - self-reporting is encouraged and focuses on system improvements rather than punitive measures.

Dose Modification: Providers may lower recommended doses if justified and documented. Reasons include liver/kidney failure, age, weight, and potential drug interactions.

FDA Pregnancy Drug Classes

Class Description
Category A No demonstrated risk to fetus in first trimester
Category B Animal studies show no risk; no adequate human studies
Category C Animal studies show adverse effects; potential benefits may warrant use
Category D Positive evidence of human fetal risk; potential benefits may warrant use
Category X Fetal abnormalities demonstrated; risks clearly outweigh benefits
