    Seizures (E003)

    Medical/Trauma Etiology

    First-Line Treatment:
    • Midazolam:
      • Adult: 5mg IV/IO/IM or 5-10mg IN (MAX 3 doses)
      • Peds: 0.1mg/kg IV/IO/IM/IN (max single dose 5mg)
    • OR Ativan (Lorazepam):
      • Adult: 2mg IV/IO/deep IM (MAX 3 doses)
      • Peds: 0.1mg/kg IV/IO/deep IM (max single dose 4mg)
    If seizure uncontrolled AND imminent respiratory compromise:
    • Consider MAAM (see MAAM guideline)
    • Use benzodiazepines for sedation post-MAAM

    Eclampsia

    • Magnesium Sulfate:
      • Adult: 5g in 50mL NS/D5W over 5-10min (NOT repeated)
    • Patient does NOT need to be actively seizing
    • Consider up to 6 weeks post-partum
    Important: Assess for occult trauma and substance abuse. In patients >50 years, seizures may be due to dysrhythmias or stroke.
