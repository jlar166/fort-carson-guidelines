    Patient Determination (A002)

    Description

    When in doubt as to whether an individual is a "patient", err on the side of caution and perform a full assessment as well as documentation.

    Criteria for Patient Status

    • Person has a complaint resulting in a call for help
    • Illness or injury suspected based on appearance or MOI
    • Person meets decision-making capacity criteria
    Important: If further care and evaluation is warranted AND the patient is refusing to be treated/transported, then a refusal MUST be completed.
