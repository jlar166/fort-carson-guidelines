  • Blood pressure: 90/60 mm/Hg to 120/80 mm/Hg
  • Breathing: 12 to 18 breaths per minute
  • Pulse: 60 to 100 beats per minute

Basic Life Support

  • Oxygen therapy - Maintain SpO2 Sat > 90% < 98%
  • Monitor vital signs
  • Splinting/wound care if clinically indicated
  • Vascular access (IV/IO) if clinically indicated
  • Transport to MOST appropriate facility

Advanced Life Support

  • All BLS Supportive care listed above
  • Cardiac monitoring when indicated
  • 12-Lead EKG assessment when indicated
  • Pain management if indicated
