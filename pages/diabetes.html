Diabetic Emergency (E007)

Hypoglycemia (<60 mg/dL)

If able to follow commands and maintain airway:
  • Oral Glucose: 1-2 full tubes (15-30g) buccal every 10min
If unable to maintain airway OR no improvement:
  • Dextrose IV/IO:
    • Adult: 12.5-25g (50mL of 50% solution)
    • Peds <13: 10mL/kg of 10% solution slowly over 10min
    • Repeat after 10min if needed until BGL >60
If vascular access unsuccessful:
  • Glucagon IM:
    • Adult: 1mg IM (NOT repeated)
    • Peds: 0.5mg IM (NOT repeated)

Hyperglycemia

  • Establish vascular access
  • Fluid Therapy: 20mL/kg NS IV/IO until improved perfusion
  • Cardiac monitor and 12-lead EKG
Special Considerations:
  • DO NOT let alcohol confuse the clinical picture - alcoholics frequently develop hypoglycemia
  • If symptoms don't resolve after treatment, consider other causes
  • Patient must eat complex carbohydrates before refusing care
