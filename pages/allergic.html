    Allergy/Anaphylaxis (E005)

    Moderate to Severe Allergic Reaction

    First-Line Treatment:
    • Epinephrine 1:1,000 IM:
      • Adult: 0.3mg (0.3mL) IM every 3-5min
      • Peds: 0.01mg/kg (0.01mL/kg) IM every 3-5min
    • Dexamethasone:
      • Adult: 10mg IV/IO/IM/PO
      • Peds: 0.5mg/kg IV/IO/IM/PO (max 10mg)
    • Diphenhydramine (Benadryl):
      • Adult: 50mg IV/IO/IM/PO
      • Peds: 1-2mg/kg IV/IO/IM/PO (max 50mg)
    If Dyspnea present:
    • Albuterol Nebulizer:
      • Adult/Peds >2: 2.5mg in 3mL NS every 10min
      • Peds <2: 1.25mg in 3mL NS
    • Consider CPAP if unresolved
    If Signs of Poor Perfusion:
    • Epinephrine Infusion: 1mg in 1,000mL NS wide open to gravity
      • Titrate to BP >90 systolic, return of distal pulses, improved mental status

    Generalized Allergic Reaction (Mild)

    • Diphenhydramine: Same dosing as above
    • General supportive care
