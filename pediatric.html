General Pediatric Overview (A006)

Definition

Pediatric patients are defined as birth until patient's weight based calculations equal the adult dose (Benzodiazepines are an exception)

Pediatric Vital Sign Ranges

Neonate (<28 days) 30-53 100-205 67-84 4.5-7
Infant (1-12 months) 30-53 100-190 72-104 9-22
Toddler (1-2 years) 22-37 98-140 86-106 22-31

Special Considerations:
  • Airways are smaller, softer, and easier to obstruct
  • Respiratory reserve is small
  • Circulatory reserve is small
  • Listen to parents - they know when changes have occurred
